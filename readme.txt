#CARU — African Artisan Jewellery



##About CARU

CARU is an African artisan jewellery brand operated by women, for the benefit and empowerment of women. The heart of CARU is love, care, and transformation.

The name CARU comes from the old English word “karo”, which evolved into “care” — to provide, protect, and care with affection and intention. In Welsh, “caru” expresses deep love and strong emotional bonds between family and friends. This spirit of love forms the foundation of everything we create.
